Other American Tyers

Following are sample flies and interesting "extras" from other notable North American tyers with flies that have significant historical value.

New pictures will be posted again in the Fall of 2009 to add flies from Charles DeFeo, Gary Howells, Alec Jackson, Darbees, Walter Dettes, and a turn of the century tie from Theodore Gordon.

Signifies Buzz Buszek FFF award winner for significant contribution to the art of fly tying.
